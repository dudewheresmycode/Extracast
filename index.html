Extracast

Extracast is a modern, hackable media player for Mac, Windows & Linux.

License

Basic Usage

  1. Download the latest build.

  2. Fire up the server.

  3. Open the player client.

  4. Select one or more video files and add them to your playlist.

  5. Connect to your Chromecast, grab a bowl of popcorn and enjoy!
